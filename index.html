Step One

Preparing for Arrival

Travel & Logistics

  • • Arrive at least 4 days early to recover from jetlag.
  • • Passport must have at least 6 months validity.
  • • ITG provides health insurance upon arrival.

Hard to Find Locally

  • Tampons (Pads are easy, tampons are difficult to buy).
  • Large Shoes (Male > size 10, Female > size 5).
  • Suncream without whiteners.
  • Strong British Tea (Yorkshire or PG Tips).

ITG Work Life

  • • Dress smartly; follow the ITG Staff Dress Code.
  • • Bring your own laptop and charger.

On the road

Getting Around HCMC

Motorbikes

Most foreigners use motorbikes, xe-ôm, or taxis.

• License: You need one for insurance and police.

• Shops: Saigon Motorcycles or DC Motorbikes.

Always wear an EU standard helmet!

Taxis & Apps

Inexpensive and very easy to use via apps.

• Apps: Grab, GoJek & Be.

• Reliable Companies: Vinasun and Mai Linh.

• Cost: Short trips ~30k VND (£1). Thao Dien to City Centre ~120k-150k VND (£4-£5).
