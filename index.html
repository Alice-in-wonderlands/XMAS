What to Pack?

The Essentials

  • Passport (valid > 6 months)
  • Laptop & Charger
  • 3-month supply of prescription meds

Hard to find in VN

  • Tampons (pads are easy, tampons are not)
  • Large Shoes (Male > size 10, Female > size 5)
  • Preferred cosmetics & Suncream

For Work (ITG)

  • Smart dress attire (Formal shoes)
  • Adapters (though they are easy to buy locally)

Airport Arrival

Visit the Visa Department (left of passport booths) before passport control.

  • • We'll arrange transport to your accommodation.
  • • We'll provide a SIM card & essentials for your first days.

Getting Around

Motorbikes

License is required for insurance. Wear an EU standard helmet!

Taxis & Apps

Use Grab, GoJek or Be. Reliable companies: Vinasun & Mai Linh.
