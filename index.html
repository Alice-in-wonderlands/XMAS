Preparing for Arrival

Travel & Health

  • Flights: Arrive at least 4 days before start date to recover from jetlag.
  • Passport: Must have > 6 months validity.
  • Health: Check general travel boosters with GP. Carry 3-month supply of prescription meds.
  • Insurance: ITG provides health insurance upon arrival. Take out travel insurance for the trip.

Work Essentials

  • ITG Clothing: Dress smartly. Check ITG Staff Dress Code.
  • Laptop: Bring your own laptop and charger.
  • Adapters: Easy to buy locally, but bring one for personal use.

Hard to find in HCMC

  • • Tampons (Pads are easy)
  • • Large Shoes (Male >10, Female >5)
  • • Female Undergarments (Size 14+ / 12+)
  • • Suncream (Locals use whiteners)
  • • British Tea (Yorkshire/PG Tips)
  • • Contact lenses/solution (Expensive)

Arriving in HCMC

Tan Son Nhat Airport

Visit the visa department before passport control (left of passport booths). We will arrange transport to your accommodation. Someone will be waiting in the arrival hall.

The First Few Days:

  • SIM Card & internet access provided.
  • Accommodation close to the center with local/western eateries.
  • Registration with local police (Landlord will assist).

Getting Around

Motorbikes & Xe-Ôm

Foreigners get around by small motorbike, xe-ôm, or taxi.

  • • License required for insurance & police.
  • • Convert UK license to VN license (easier than a test).
  • • Wear an EU standard helmet.
  • • Recommended shops: Saigon Motorcycles, DC Motorbikes.

Taxis & Apps

Inexpensive and easy. Use Grab, GoJek & Be.

Reliable Companies: Vinasun & Mai Linh.

Cost: ~30k VND (short trip) to 150k VND (Thao Dien to city centre).

Thao Dien

Expat Bubble

District 2. Serene, upscale, trendy boutiques and gourmet restaurants.

Rental Rules to Remember

  • Contract: Must be in writing. Request bilingual version.
  • Deposit: Equivalent to 2 months' rent. Pay in VND.
  • Utilities: Electricity, water, internet usually extra.
  • Lease: Usually 1 year, flexible.
  • Notice: 1-3 months notice for termination.
  • Police: Landlord assists with residency registration.
